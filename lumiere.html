Lumière

Created as a conceptual skincare brand, Lumière is a company that focuses on the use of wholesome, natural ingredients. The brand is meant to appeal to millenials espeically, with its clean and simple branding. The use of teal and yellow were decided upon to evoke a sense of cleanliness and happiness. The last few images included are from the brand identity manual.
