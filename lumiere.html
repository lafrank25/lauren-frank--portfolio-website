Lumière

Created as a conceptual skincare brand, Lumière is a company that focuses on the use of wholesome, natural ingredients. The brand is meant to appeal to all women, but specifically to millenials, with its clean and simple branding. The use of teal and yellow were decided upon to evoke a sense of cleanliness and happiness. The last three images are from the brand's identity manual.
