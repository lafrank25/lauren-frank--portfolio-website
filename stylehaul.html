stylehaul

From January to March 2018, I worked as a Graphic Design Intern for StyleHaul, a media company based in Los Angeles. As an intern, I created graphics for the company's social media accounts, as well as designed YouTube banner ideas for StyleHaul creators, banners for company-wide emails, presentation decks, and a layout of the company values, later printed onto a glass sheet and hung in the StyleHaul headquarters.
