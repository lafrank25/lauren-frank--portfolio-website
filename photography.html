photography

Rooted in an interest in the history of graphic design, this project attempts to break down both art and design to their most simple things: color and composition. The Bauhaus, Swiss Design, and de Stijl all focus on large blocks of color taking up the majority of a space. These compositions were created using colored paper, fogged plexiglass, and a mirror.
